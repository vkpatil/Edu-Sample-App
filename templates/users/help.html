    Quick Start

    1. Open your Dashboard to see your role-specific summary.
    2. Visit Courses to browse or manage course content.
    3. Use Enrollments to track learner status.
    4. Use Quizzes for assessments and results.
    5. Keep your information updated in My Profile.

    Courses

    Browse course catalog, view course owners, and access course-specific actions based on your role.

    Enrollments

    Students self-enroll; teaching and admin roles can manage enrollment records and status updates.

    Quizzes

    Attempt timed quizzes, navigate question-by-question, and review score details after submission.

    Account

    Use My Profile to update personal details. Admin users can also create staff and student users.

    When Access Is Denied

    1. Confirm you are signed in with the correct account.
    2. Check your role badge in the top bar.
    3. Compare your role with the access table above.
    4. If access still seems incorrect, contact an Admin or Super Admin.

    Access Levels At a Glance

    Use this quick summary to understand who can grant what:

    • Student: self-service learning and quiz participation.
    • Teacher: manages own-course content and learner progress.
    • Admin: operational control and user provisioning.
    • Super Admin: full platform oversight and governance.
